Acne skin benefits from salicylic acid cleansers and oil-free moisturizers.

Niacinamide helps reduce acne inflammation and oil production.

People with medium skin tone should avoid harsh alcohol-based products.

For acne-prone skin, dermatologists recommend:
- Salicylic acid cleanser
- Niacinamide serum
- Oil-free moisturizer
- Sunscreen SPF 50

Dry skin needs hyaluronic acid and ceramide moisturizers.

Oily skin should use lightweight gel moisturizers and niacinamide serums.
